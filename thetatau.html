PROFESSIONAL COED ENGINEERING FRATERNITY

@ Texas A&M University

Brotherhood


Without brotherhood there is no fraternity. Through weekly events, intramurals, field days, long study hous and more, we build lifelong bonds amongst our members. As a national fraternity, you'll have brothers across the country.

Professionalism


As an engineering fraternity, we take great pride in our work and in our careers. Through resume sessions, mock interviews and shared experiences, we prepare ourselves and each other for the journey to success.

Philanthropy


Xi Gamma gives back to the Bryan, College Station community. Throughout the year we visit food banks, host STEM nights for elementary, middle, and high schools and help keep our communities clean. We also participate in the yearly city-wide community service day, Big Event.

Why are we on campus?


We strive to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among its members and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.


Xi Gamma is about building a career you can be proud of and making lifelong friends along the way. We push and support each other to become the best that we can be.

Application Requirement

  • Engineering Major
  • Not within a semester of graduation.
  • Not a member of a competing fraternity

Membership Requirements

  • Have at least a GPA of 2.0 at time of initiation
  • With your pledge class,
    • organize a professional development event.
    • Organize a community service event.
  • Attend the MANDATORY pledge meeting every Sunday at 6:04.
  • Build a Theta Tau hammer.
  • Attend Big/Little event.
  • Take and pass the pledge exams.
  • About 1 activity per week.
  • Complete pledge tasks.
  • Attend mandatory events.
  • Pay your dues:
    • Dues per semester: $300
    • Can be paid all at once or in $50 installments.
    • Fees cover paid events, activities, national dues, food, etc.
